# Opus Match AI (MyLong IO, Inc.)

## What We Do
Opus Match AI is a bootstrapped, profitable AI-powered candidate matching and staffing technology platform. We integrate natively with Bullhorn ATS and serve healthcare staffing agencies. Our technology uses machine learning to match qualified healthcare candidates with facilities through human-centric, user-first technology.

## Who Our Customers Are
Enterprise healthcare staffing agencies with $100M+ revenue. We operate on a shared cost model.

## Active Clients
Host Healthcare, GHR Healthcare, HAN Staff, Concentric Healthcare, WWHS (Worldwide Health Staff), CrossMed, Focus Staff (Elite365), Pioneer Healthcare, Recovered Health.

## Key Integration
Native Bullhorn ATS partnership (Marketplace certified). This is our most critical technical asset.

## Values
- Human-centric, people-first culture
- User-first technology design
- Bootstrapped and profitable — sustainable unit economics
